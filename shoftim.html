A Mishpat Ivri Adjudication DAO

Kinyan Sudar

A “kinyan sudar” is a contract that moves a question or object to the juridisdiction of the DAO.

Shtar Beirurin

A “shtar berurin” is a contract that gives and defines the scope and power of the DAO to rule on questions and disputes.
